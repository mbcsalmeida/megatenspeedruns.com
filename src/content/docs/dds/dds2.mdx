## Speedrun History
<Tabs>
    <TabItem label="Normal Mode w/o Data Transfer">
        <Steps>
            1. Shin Megami Tensei: Digital Devil Saga 2 was originally released worldwide by March 17, 2007.
            2. The first recorded run was completed by Kanri on 2016/06/29 with a time of [**6:38:39**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/75217182).
            3. On 2017/03/08, runner KMB_Chimera improved the world record to a time of [**5:25:52**](https://youtu.be/KHk1pWwnWoI)
            4. On 2017/04/19, runner Otogitune improved the world record to a time of [**5:21:00**](https://youtu.be/eWseqZ659J0)
            5. On 2017/09/02, Kanri got back the world record with a the first sub 5h run, [**4:59:39**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/171613321)
            4. After nearly 5 years, on 2022/05/13, Freedom_Pulse improved the world record to a time of [**4:56:38**](https://youtu.be/kVZIZmqB0uM)
            5. On 2024/11/20, Jonperi23 beat the 2 year old record with a time of [**4:52:10**](https://youtu.be/Q5RQTGDrL18)
            6. After a thorough reroute and multiple attempts, mizusima40 showcased the improvements of his route with a now new world record of [**4:32:36**](https://youtu.be/UDa3LeanJkc)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="Hard Mode, All Bosses">
        <Steps>
        1. Shin Megami Tensei: Digital Devil Saga 2 was originally released worldwide by March 17, 2007.
        2. The first recorded run was completed by Kanri on 2018/08/09 with a time of [**15:59:54**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/294945452)
        3. On 2023/01/05, Freedom_Pulse improved the world record by two hours to [**13:52:42**](https://youtu.be/W1v9DgvS6KQ)
        4. In 2023, known accomplished router and runner Shaggy3311 started improving the route and finished his first world record with a time of [**12:20:57**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1952253332)
        5. The current world record is held by Shaggy3311 with a time of [**9:30:49**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2345484629)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>
