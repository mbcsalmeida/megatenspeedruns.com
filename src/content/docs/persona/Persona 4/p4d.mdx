## Speedrun History
<Steps>
    1. Persona 4: Dancing All Night  was released in Japan on June 25, 2015.
    2. The first ever recorded run was done by Ceidian with a time of [**2:04:39**](https://youtu.be/9IcrQTQ3Oxo)
    3. On 2021/04/19, Lonne beat the world record with a time of [**2:02:32**](https://youtu.be/ARjXE-RSYns) which he later improved to [**2:01:37**](https://youtu.be/t65XfmhAXwc) 
    2. The current world record holder is asapaska with a time of [**2:01:16**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1669409818?t=2h26m6s)
</Steps>
