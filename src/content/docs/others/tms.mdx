## Speedrun History

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="Hard, NG - Wii U">
<Steps>
1. Tokyo Mirage Sessions #FE was released in Japan on December 26, 2015.
2. The first recorded speedrun was done by O-den on 2017/05/03 with a time of [**11:12:55**](https://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm31141771)
3. On 2018/04/29 legrandgrand improved the world record to a time of [**11:15:36**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OVC-2SQk8iM) with improvements on HeroMystic's route.
4. On 2018/05/06, HeroMystic took back the world record with a time of [**10:30:45**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/258238938).
5. 0n 2018/05/09, legrandgrand reclaimed the world record with a time of [**9:12:15](https://youtu.be/noBNKezb4TI).
6. The current world record is [**8:52:42**](https://youtu.be/HIhQ-qu3Oi8) held by legrandgrand.
</Steps>
</TabItem>

<TabItem label="Hard, NG - Switch">
<Steps>
1. Tokyo Mirage Sessions #FE Encore was released in Japan on January 17, 2020.
2. The first recorded speedrun was done by legrandgrand with a time of [**8:29:58**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qwoLPWnImPg).
3. The current world record is held by legrandgrand with a time of [**7:17:21**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GkbBXwmth0w).
</Steps>
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
