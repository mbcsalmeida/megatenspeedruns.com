## Speedrun History

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="Megami Tensei">
<Steps>
1. Kyuuyaku Megami Tensei was released in Japan on March 31, 1995.
2. The first available recorded speedrun was done by めし太郎 on 2013/08/11 with a time of [**4:05:29**](https://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm21565155).
3. On 2018/01/01, Darko uploaded a run with a time of [**2:11:44**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/293976962), close to halving the time of the previous record.
4. On 2018/01/11, ten days later, equi completed a run with his riskier route and achieved a time of [**1:28:23**](https://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm32565606). This time stood as the world record for 4 years.
5. On 2022/11/13, accomplished megaten runner mulsqi achieved a new world record with a time of [**1:19:27**](https://youtu.be/04lfFz0MtBc).
6. Mulsqi further improve the record on 2023/09/06 to a time of [**1:05:06**](https://youtu.be/gi0m56nHHiY). On 2025/08/17, mulsqi finally achieved the first sub 1 hour time with a [**59:14**](https://youtu.be/hqjj7M8pSZc).
7. On 2025/08/18, a day later, mizusima40 stole the world record from mulsqi for the first time in 3 years, achieving a time of [**57:02**](https://youtu.be/uIMc9iNto7A) with his risky Odin route.
8. The current world record is held by mulsqi, achieved on September 1st 2025, with a time of [**56:45**](https://youtu.be/LpvkfAzRG2Y). The fight for the fastest time is still very much active with a new route in the makings.
</Steps>
</TabItem>
