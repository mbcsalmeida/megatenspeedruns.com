## Speedrun History

<Tabs>
    <TabItem label="SNES - Yumi">
        <Steps>
            1. Shin Megami Tensei If... was released in Japan on October 28, 1994.
            2. The first recorded run for the Yumi Route was done by korosuke with a time of [**3:02:12**](https://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm4278579)
            3. On 2018/11/18, Symm achieved a new world record of [**1:52:34**](https://youtu.be/jpRJI88N1Ak)
            4. On 2019/06/01, mizusima40 achieved a new world record of [**1:30:16**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/382656890)
            5. On 2021/01/14, Symm reclaimed the world record of [**1:30:15**](https://youtu.be/XJUBxrIB-0o)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="SNES - Akira">
        <Steps>
            1. Shin Megami Tensei If... was released in Japan on October 28, 1994.
            2. The first recorded run for the Akira Route was done by ドミネーター with a time of [**2:09:51**](https://youtu.be/XJUBxrIB-0o)
            3. On 2017/04/23, kakkun achieved a new record of [**2:00:05**](https://youtu.be/w-e6EiCBUi8)
            4. On 2019/07/01, mizusima40 achieved a new record of [**1:50:18**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/405448859)
            5. On 2023/03/17, pipio attempted new route adjustments and beat the Akira world record that had been standing for 4 years with a time of [**1:45:50**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1767631900)
            6. On 2024/11/09, mizusima40 reclaimed the world record with a time of [**1:45:05**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2297346734)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="PS1 - Yumi">
        <Steps>
            1. Shin Megami Tensei If... was released in Japan on December 26, 2002.
            2  The first ever recorded run of the PS1 version of If... was done on 2011/03/04 by TA539, completing Charlie's route on EASY difficulty with no restrictions, with a time of [**1:39:14**](https://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm13781864)
            3. The first ever Yumi route run was done by equi on 2020/08/24 on EASY difficulty, with no restrictions, with a time of [**1:12:34**](http://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm33552720)
            3. On 2021/08/29, R-0109 ran Yumi route on NORMAL difficulty with restrictions and achieved a time of [**1:30:13**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1137351030)
            4. On 2023/02/15, ToxicTT ran Yumi on EASY difficulty with restrictions and achieved a time of [**1:21:15**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1737221310)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="PS1 - Akira">
        <Steps>
            1. Shin Megami Tensei If... was released in Japan on December 26, 2002.
            2. The first ever run and current world record was done by equi on 2018/07/19 on EASY difficulty with no restrictions with a time of [**1:16:09**](http://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm37411190)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>
