## Speedrun History
<Tabs>
    <TabItem label="Hard TNE">
        <Steps>
            1. Shin Megami Tensei V was released worldwide on November 11, 2021.
            2. The first completed run was done on 2021/12/31 by romulostx with a time of [**12:40:45**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1248653824). This time would be improved to a time of [**9:53:27**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1449363167)
            3. On 2022/05/28, Shaggy3311 improved the record to a time of [**9:29:02**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1311852027) and brought it down to a time of [**8:24:14**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1582026223)
            4. On 2022/11/25, asapaska improved the world record to a time of [**8:10:56**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1663222487)
            5. On 2023/01/02, Shaggy3311 improved the world record with a time of [**7:25:44**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1696104648)
            6. Following the release of the Nintendo Switch 2 on 2025/06/05, on 2025/06/07, asapaska improved the world record with a time of [**7:10:48**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H0s24395HDQ)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="Safety Any%">
        <Steps>
        1. Shin Megami Tensei V was released worldwide on November 11, 2021.
        2. The first completed run was done on 2021/11/27 by secretbunnyninja with a time of [**5:09:22**](https://youtu.be/6jNkOEN_jak)
        3. On 2021/1203, Engiki improved to a time of [**3:05:45**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1235172003). The vod is no longer available.
        4. 5 days later, sioneus improved the record to a time of [**2:41:55**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1227257096)
        5. 8 days later, Engiki reclaimed the record to a time of [**2:40:34**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1235172003)
        6. On 2022/01/22, sioneus reclaimed the record to a time of [**2:32:22**](https://youtu.be/QRTuZuuBN2U)
        7. 1 day later, DarkRedDove set a new record of [**2:30:30**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1274550863)
        8. At ESA Summer 2022, sioneus achieved a new time with some route improvements and reached a time of [**2:22:28**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1542182099)
        9. On 2022/09/20, asapaska achieved a new time with a [**2:17:53**](https://youtu.be/uGDf9znO1Gw)
        10. 2 years later, after the release of the Nintendo Switch 2, ToxicTT updated the record to a [**2:11:43**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UBDSq4tr28M)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>
