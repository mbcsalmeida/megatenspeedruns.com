## Speedrun History
<Steps>
1. Devil Summoner - Raidou Kuzunoha vs. King Abaddon was released in Japan on August 30, 2012 and worldwide by September 26, 2013.
2. The first ever recorded run was done on 2017/05/18 by 運の香, read UnnoKou, with a time of [**7:24:59**](https://youtu.be/jVc8MkL5pZ4).
3. The current world record was done by ExcelitoL with a time of [**6:56:18**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TFFGGP7KrbE).
