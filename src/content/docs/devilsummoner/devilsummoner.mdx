## Speedrun History
<Tabs>
<TabItem label="Any% - PSP">
<Steps>
1. Devil Summoner was released in Japan on December 22, 2005.
2. The first recorded speedrun was done by sioneus on 2021/08/25 with a time of **3:10:57** later improved to [**2:39:09**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1129250187)
3. On 2023/12/22, ToxicTT showcased his new manip route and obtained a time of [**1:49:12**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HUKmUdm-TI4)
4. The current world record is held by ToxicTT done on 2024/06/12 with a time of [**1:22:09**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cncnqb98u7c), holding the emulator world record as well with a time of [**1:12:03**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i5eO6FJuIIw)
</Steps>
</TabItem>

<TabItem label="Any% - Sega Saturn">
<Steps>
1. Devil Summoner was released in Japan on December 25, 1995.
2. There are details of a Sega Saturn run by [hirosue_peer](http://blog.livedoor.jp/hirosue_peer/archives/51923552.html#more) with an alleged time of **** but not recording.
3. The current world record seems to be [**5:00:44**](https://youtu.be/mSqw5wxS4kg?si=D1GYFKgOmtiZCjWv) by Ucchare.
</Steps>
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
